The Important Rules

Lets keep the commmunity safer together. Please follow the rules, or you may lose the ability to sell on Kraya.

  • Ship within 2 business days 🚢

    Please ship items within 2 business days after a show or when an item is sold.

  • Package items safely 📦

    Package items you sell with care and in protective material to ensure they don't get damaged.

  • Be nice ✌️

    Be sure you treat everyone with respect and don't bully anyone in the community.

  • Don't lie about an item 🤥

    Don't mislead a buyer about an item's value, condition, or anything else.

  • Authentic Product ✅

    Sell genuine products. Counterfeit, fake, or pirated items should not be listed or promoted.
